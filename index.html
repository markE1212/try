Partnership

Meet our partners

At Klengers Co, we partner with industry leaders like Petronas, Telekom Malaysia, Microsoft, and AWS. Petronas excels in sustainable energy management, while Telekom Malaysia enhances connectivity nationwide with advanced ICT solutions. Microsoft drives innovation with software and cloud solutions, and AWS provides scalable infrastructure for efficient operations. These partnerships enable Klengers Co to deliver cutting-edge solutions across sectors, empowering businesses to thrive in a digital-first world.

Petronas

Petronas (Petroliam Nasional Berhad) is Malaysia's state-owned oil and gas company, established in 1974. It operates in upstream exploration, production, downstream refining, marketing, and petrochemicals.

Telekom Malaysia

Telekom Malaysia (TM) is Malaysia's largest telecommunications company, offering fixed-line, mobile, broadband, and ICT services. Known for its Unifi high-speed broadband, TM plays a key role in the nation's telecom infrastructure.

Microsoft

Microsoft is an American tech giant founded in 1975, famous for its Windows OS, Office suite, and Azure cloud services. It also produces hardware like Xbox and Surface devices, and owns LinkedIn.

Amazon Web Services

Amazon Web Services (AWS), a subsidiary of Amazon, provides cloud computing services including storage, computing power, and databases. Launched in 2006, AWS is a leader in the cloud industry, known for scalability and innovation.
